# Objective
Analyze retail sales data to gain actionable insights that will enhance the performance of the coffee shop.

# Recommended Analysis
- How do sales vary by day of the week & hour of the day?
- Are there any peak times for sales activity?
- What is the total sales revenue for each month?
- How do sales vary across different stores locations?
- What is the average price/order per person?
- Which products are the best-selling in terms of quantity & revenue?
- How do sales vary by product category & type?
